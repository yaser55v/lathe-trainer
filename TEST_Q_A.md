In case of sudden breakdowns, what do we do?
⎘
Right, the first step is to hit the emergency stop button at the control panel or foot switch to bring the machine to a complete halt. Then make sure the safety door is fully closed and the interlock is engaged. Wait for the machine to power down completely and for the indicator lights to show it is safe before doing anything else. Do not attempt to restart or clear the fault until the root cause is identified and a qualified technician has cleared the machine. This follows the ISO 13850 emergency‑stop protocol.
