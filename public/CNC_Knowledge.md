# CNC Turning Center — Machine Specifications

## Physical Dimensions
- Overall footprint: 2.8m × 2.2m × 2.4m (L×W×H)
- Machine weight: ~3,200 kg
- Floor-mounted with integrated chip conveyor

## Spindle Specifications
- Maximum speed: 4,000 RPM
- Chuck type: 3-jaw hydraulic power chuck
- Bar capacity: 65mm diameter maximum
- Spindle motor: 22 kW continuous

## Tool Turret
- Configuration: 12-station servo turret
- Live tool positions: 6 stations (milling/drilling capable)
- Indexing time: <0.2 seconds
- Tool change time: <0.5 seconds (station-to-station)

## Control System
- CNC controller: Fanuc 0i-TF series
- Programming: ISO G-code, conversational, and MDI modes
- Display: 10.4" color LCD touchscreen
- Interfaces: USB, Ethernet, RS-232

## Axes & Performance
- X-axis travel: 250mm (cross-slide)
- Z-axis travel: 500mm (longitudinal)
- Positioning accuracy: ±0.005mm
- Repeatability: ±0.002mm

## Safety Systems
- Automatic sliding safety door with interlocks
- Emergency stop (E-Stop) at control panel and optional foot switch
- Chip guarding and full enclosure
- Coolant interlock (prevents door opening during high-pressure cycle)

## Workpiece Capacity
- Maximum turning diameter: 350mm
- Maximum turning length: 500mm
- Bar work capacity: 65mm diameter
- Chuck work capacity: 250mm diameter

## Operational Features
- Through-spindle coolant: 70 bar high-pressure
- Standard flood coolant: 15 liters/min
- Chip conveyor: continuous belt, auto-discharge
- Live tooling speed: up to 6,000 RPM (milling spindle)

## Maintenance Intervals
- Daily: chip conveyor clear, coolant level check
- Weekly: way lubrication inspection, tool turret cleaning
- Monthly: hydraulic pressure verification, spindle bearing temperature check
- Annual: full alignment calibration, ball screw inspection
